About Us:

As the name implies, “ Your Future Job ” explores how we use visualization to understand specific academic data to help to make a decision ourselves. We are students from Uni-Due and this project work is submitted in fulfillment of requirement for practical project (Praxis projekt) in Msc. Computer Engineering spec(Intelligent Networked Systems) under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Mohamed Amine Chatti with a focus on visualization for presenting data to everyone. This is an interactive visualization application for people who want a more clear view of on going trends of studies and their market values. The application visualizes different aspects of the study programs such as yearly enrollments, salary in market and unemployment rate. Users can also compare approximate salary after completing the different courses. After interacting with the application the user should have a bit of clearer understanding of selection of subject according to different attributes if user isn’t clear in his option about a specific subject.
